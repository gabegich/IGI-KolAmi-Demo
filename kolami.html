The Fund

About Kol Ami

The Kol Ami Student Managed Investment Fund is the flagship program of Investment Group Israel at Reichman University. Selected through a competitive process, fund members conduct fundamental equity research, present investment theses, and manage a real portfolio through a committee-based decision structure. The fund maintains a concentrated, high-conviction portfolio of global equities with a focus on identifying asymmetric risk-reward opportunities across sectors and geographies.
