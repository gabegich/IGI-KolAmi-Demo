Our Mission

Bridging the gap between
classroom and market

Investment Group Israel (IGI) is Reichman University's premier student-led investment organization. Founded in 20XX, IGI provides a rigorous platform for students passionate about capital markets, equities, and investment management. Through structured educational programs, industry guest speakers, stock pitch competitions, and our flagship student managed fund, IGI bridges the gap between academic theory and professional practice.
